### Bill progress bar
Added a visual 6-step progress bar to the top of each bill page, showing how far a bill has progressed through the legislative process.

**New function:** `render_bill_progress_bar(status)` in `R/utils.R`

- Steps: Introduced → Passed Subcommittee → Passed Committee → Passed One Chamber → Passed Both Chambers → Signed by Governor
- Completed steps are highlighted blue (`#1a56db`); remaining steps are grey

### Legislator–lobbyist alignment score
New pipeline script: `R/data_pipeline/04a_legislator_lobbyist_alignment.R`

**Methodology:**
- For each (legislator, client) pair, computes what fraction of floor passage votes the legislator cast in alignment with the client's declared position
- Aligned: client "For" + legislator Yea, or client "Against" + legislator Nay
- Opposed: the reverse
- "Undecided" and "Withdrawn" declarations are excluded
- Only floor passage votes used (filtered by `str_detect(vote_desc, "bill pass")`)
- Minimum 5 shared bill-votes required (`MIN_SHARED_BILLS = 5`)
- One bill can contribute two bill-votes if it passed both chambers (n_shared counts bill-votes, not unique bills)

**Output:** `data/lobbying/legislator_client_alignment.rds`
- Columns: `people_id`, `client`, `n_shared`, `n_aligned`, `n_opposed`, `alignment`, `rank_type` ("most_aligned"/"most_opposed"), `rank`, `name`, `party`, `role`, `district`
- 1,502 rows (top 5 most aligned + top 5 most opposed per legislator)

**Pipeline stats (91st GA as of this session):**
- 41,211 floor Yea/Nay votes | 155 legislators | 345 bills
- 15,070 For/Against declarations | 846 clients | 2,784 bills
- 230,879 (legislator, client, bill-vote) triples after join
- 13,901 valid pairs before top-N filtering

**Spot checks:** Results are ideologically coherent across party lines (Wahls/Scholten aligning with Sierra Club, mental health advocates; Gerhold/Zumbach aligning with Family Leader, Americans for Prosperity).

**Not yet integrated into legislator template** — holding off until further review of the data.
